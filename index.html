  • Real-world experience: They don’t care how much you’ve practiced; they care if you’ve delivered in a professional environment.
  • Corporate dynamics: If you don’t understand workflows, team collaboration, and deadlines, you’re a liability, not an asset.
  • Referrals and connections: In today’s job market, who you know matters just as much as what you know.
